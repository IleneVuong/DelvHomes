Technology

Technology is heavily incorporated into the homes to leave residents with the best experience. The technology is designed, installed, and maintained by Delve Homes Tech team to ensure full access to one of the most important aspects of their homes. In doing so, Delve Homes is able to provide the latest model of 3D video chats, high-quality sound systems built right into the home, intricately designed isolation mode and summoning buttons for everything in the home. Constantly updating and making improvements, the technology is guaranteed to perform perfectly every second of every day without fail. Our energy-efficient homes have all been recently upgraded throughout, with 41 paid power panels and enhancements including UV blind systems, custom drapes, closets, and built-in machinery.

History

After the drastic event of deteriorating ozone, the Machine became installed in all our lives. Public gatherings have long since been abandoned. Reaching an age where people are left with no other choice but to remain underground and indoors. Interacting via technology, living a virtual rather than face-to-face existence in both education and socializing. Delve Homes plans to unite all of these aspects together, providing an affordable and considerable living environment for your best interest.
