Amenities

Amenities Provided

Health Care

The Machine monitors residents' every move to respond accordingly to their needs. This of course includes when residents' temperature rises or lowers, frequent coughs, abnormal perspiration, or any other concerning factors.

Isolation Mode

When activated drops private shutters that block any connection to the outside world. This ties along with the belief that everyone deserves privacy. Whether it’s to disconnect for a bit or relieve any sort of stress.

Restful Ease

Summon a levitating bed with a click of a button as we believe rest is vital for better mental health, increased concentration and memory, and overall a healthier immune system.

Experiences Available Underground

Visit our Bungalow Underground Gardens, see a man-made network of underground rooms, courtyards, and passageways reminiscent of our ancestors prior to The Machine. Includes unique fruit-producing trees and vines growing underground, some being over 200 years old! Includes the visit to the underground home of Basque visionary and self-taught artist of the gardens.

Airship Travel

Within the airship, experience the surface, see diverse landscapes, oceansides, and much more. Get to see over 600,000 species of plants and fungi, and other unfathomable details about the land we used to inhabit. Many cities have become completely bare, don’t forget to visit the numerous ghost towns labeled on the airship radar. Our most populated experience being the Great San Tito Mountain tour, this being a half-mile hike from the main crater to reach the remaining town, which was built in 1890 near a mine with gold, silver, copper, and more.
