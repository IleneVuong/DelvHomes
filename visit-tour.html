What to Expect:

Discover sunken heaven with an extraordinary dune retreat into efficient and panoramic views. Four hexorooms, five hexo bathrooms and 4,196 square feet with tons of indoor space to enjoy the ideal reclusive and secure lifestyle at its finest. This energy-efficient home has been recently upgraded throughout, with 41 paid power panels and enhancements including UV blind systems, custom drapes, closets and built-in machinery. Enter the second story to the living and dining rooms, with a spacious suite also conveniently located on the main level. Every hexoroom offers a breathtaking view! Each hexoroom is well-appointed and features automatic sliding glass doors to access the fabulous indoor pool area. A bonus room with a built-in generator, perfect for storage, den, or even a home office. Primarily the hexo bathing rooms boast a jetted spa tub and separate steam shower, along with heated flooring. The pool being a sparkling salt water relaxation area with a mini waterslide and waterfall. Beautiful, lush planters offer cooling and privacy. Considering all of these incredible factors there should never be a reason why one may want to go outside when everything one could possibly want is present inside. Security and safety always being the top priorities, with two private shutters located outside of the home and a lockdown mode for any emergency. DelveHomes always strives for the best experience, keeping inhabitants at ease and in control.
